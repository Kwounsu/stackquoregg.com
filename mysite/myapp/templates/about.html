Introduction of the Stack Quoregg

The website is the final project, Kwounsu Lee has created to pass the web programming class, CINS465.

Nothing to tell you more..

Oh! And this is how the logo of Stack Quoregg created.

There are two main contents in this website to help people share their knowledge.

You can post questions and answer to them by comments in "Q & A". Upvote for the good answers to give them credit!

You can also ask questions to people with chatting in "Tutor". People can enter the chat room through tutor registration post in Tutor.

But this is not a individual separated chat room. Becuase the spirit of Stack Quoregg is 'to share knowledge'!

Therefore, everyone will be joined to the chat and the conversations will be gone.
